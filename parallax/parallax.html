Bucket List Travel

Travel should be meaningful. It should excite and inspire you, rejuvenate and ground you, educate and challenge you, and most of all, it should humble you. Travel gives us our greatest stories, our most cherished memories and countless irreplaceable learnings that we can choose to pay forward to others. It teaches us about ourselves and each other, it broadens our horizons and, just like a reset button, it forces us to refocus on what really matters.

Rome

Rome is Europe's third most visited city and the fifteenth in the globe. It draws visitors from all over the world who are eager to see the city's magnificent structures and archaeological sites, as well as its renowned food and vibrant environment.

London

London is a fantastic place to visit, especially thanks to England's excellent public transit. You could easily spend a week or more experiencing everything that each London area has to offer, and still have money left over for your next trip.

Paris

Paris is unlike any other city in the world. It's brimming with culture, history, and natural beauty. While many visitors come to Paris to see the Louvre, the Eiffel Tower, or Notre-Dame, the real magic is found in the streets. A journey to Paris would be a once in a lifetime experience.

Tokyo

The city of Tokyo is the world's most populous. Because English is not widely spoken, globalization's impact is limited. It's a breath of fresh air amid the many destinations that have become lost in their efforts to pander to tourists and achieve global standards.

Dubai

Dubai is loaded with contrasts. There's the sparkling horizon, consisting of high rises so tall they oppose creative minds; the advanced assortment of stores and diners, presenting each sort of choice an explorer might actually need; and the extravagant inns, prepared to welcome even the most insightful voyager in style and overabundance.

Bali

The most fulfilling and exciting Bali vacations include several places, so you don't have to worry about choosing just one atmosphere or scene. If you don't have enough time, it's best to combine some or two. It doesn't make sense to rush to a temple tour, taste experience, yoga session, shopping, or Balinese massage. Enjoy your time on the island and the overwhelmingly generous, sweet and authentic locals. Taking all the factors into account, Bali feels much better, happier, healthier, and arguably almost impossible to stay tanned than when landing.

Florence

This is a city surrounded by nature like no other. Cross the Ponte Vecchio and the river Arno to reach the Ortoralno district, with its cobblestone squares, cozy cafes and centuries-old craftsmen. Take a walk along the riverbank, explore the exquisite Renaissance gardens, or meander through cypress-covered hills. Every turn, you get a spectacular view of the city and its huge terracotta dome, as opposed to the mountains in the distance. In Florence, even landscape is an art.
